PRIVACY POLICY

Radical Transparency. Your data, secured by design.

Last Updated: December 2025

01. The Protocol

At Lux & Taurus ("we", "us", or "our"), we believe privacy is a fundamental right, not a feature. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclosure, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. We do not sell your data. We do not track you unnecessarily.

02. Information Collection

We collect only the data necessary to provide our high-precision services:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, and company details provided voluntarily via our contact forms.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and operating system for security diagnostics and optimization.
  • Project Data: Specifications, assets, and credentials you share with us to execute your project.

03. Usage of Intelligence

We utilize the collected information to:

  • Engineer and deliver your requested digital products.
  • Communicate regarding project milestones and updates.
  • Optimize our website performance and security architecture.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

04. Security Measures

We implement military-grade encryption and security-first coding practices to protect your personal information. However, please be aware that no transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

05. Third-Party Systems

We may employ third-party companies (e.g., payment processors, hosting services) to facilitate our Service. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
